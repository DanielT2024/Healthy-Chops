About Us

Savor the flavor, nourish your soul: that's the Healthy Chops way!

Welcome to Healthy Chops

At Healthy Chops, we're passionate about serving up delicious, nutritious food that fuels your active lifestyle. Our story began with a simple yet powerful idea: that healthy eating shouldn't have to mean sacrificing flavor or convenience.

At Healthy Chops, we're committed to:

  • Quality: We use only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients, sourced from local farmers and suppliers whenever possible.
  • Innovation: We're constantly experimenting with new flavors, ingredients, and cooking techniques to stay ahead of the curve.
  • Sustainability: We're dedicated to reducing our environmental footprint through eco-friendly packaging, sustainable sourcing, and responsible business practices..
  • We believe that healthy eating should be accessible to everyone, regardless of dietary restrictions or preferences. That's why we offer a range of products that cater to different needs and tastes.

Beans Flour

Our all-natural beans flour is renowned for its smooth texture, natural taste, and consistent granular formation; and contains no added preservatives or artificial flavorings. It is a great source of dietary fiber, protein, iron, and many other essential nutrients. This versatile flour can be used in a variety of recipes, from baked goods to savory dishes. It can be used as a thickener in soups and stews, or as a creamy dip or filling.

Why Choose Our Beans Flour?

  • High in Protein: Our beans flour is an excellent source of plant-based protein, making it an ideal choice for vegetarians and vegans
  • Gluten-Free:Perfect for those with gluten intolerance or sensitivity, our beans flour is a great alternative to traditional flours.
  • Rich in Fiber:With a high fiber content, our beans flour can help support healthy digestion and satiety.
  • Low on the Glycemic Index:Our beans flour is slowly digested, making it a great choice for those managing blood sugar levels.
  • Versatile: Use our beans flour to make a variety of dishes, from baked goods to savory meals, and as a thickener or dip.
  • No Additives or Preservatives:Our beans flour is made from 100% natural ingredients, with no added preservatives or artificial flavorings.

Ijebu-Garri

Ijebu Garri, also known as cassava flakes, is a popular West African staple food made from cassava tubers. Our Ijebu Garri is carefully processed to preserve the natural flavor and nutrients of the cassava, resulting in a delicious and crunchy snack that is rich in carbohydrates, fiber, and essential minerals.

Nutritional Benefits of Ijebu Garri

  • Rich in Carbohydrates: Ijebu Garri is an excellent source of energy, making it a great snack for those looking for a quick pick-me-up.
  • High in Fiber: Our Ijebu Garri is rich in dietary fiber, which can help support healthy digestion and satiety.
  • Good Source of Minerals: Ijebu Garri is a good source of essential minerals such as potassium, magnesium, and iron.
  • Gluten-Free: Our Ijebu Garri is naturally gluten-free, making it an excellent choice for those with gluten intolerance or sensitivity.
